A place to work

Not just a café. Not just an office.

The Little Place is designed for people who need focus — freelancers, remote workers, entrepreneurs, and students who've outgrown their kitchen table but don't need a full office lease.

We've thought about every detail that affects your productivity: the noise level, the chair height, the light, the WiFi speed, and yes — the quality of the coffee.

High-speed dedicated fiber internet (500 Mbps)
Ergonomic seating and standing desk options
Quiet zones and collaboration zones
Private phone booth for calls
Printing and scanning included with membership

A place to belong

The professional community of Miraflores.

One of the real costs of remote work is isolation. The Little Place is built to fix that — without forcing it. You work at your own pace, and the community is there when you want it.

Monthly networking evenings, skill-share workshops, and a members-only WhatsApp group connect you to other professionals working in the same space.
